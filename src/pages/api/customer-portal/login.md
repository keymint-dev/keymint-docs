# 👤 Customer Login

Customers can securely access their license details through the Keymint Customer Dashboard.

---

## 🚪 How Login Works

Follow these steps to log in as a customer:

1. Go to [**app.keymint.dev**](https://app.keymint.dev).
2. Click the **Customer** tab on the login screen.
3. Enter the **Vendor Code** and your **Email**.

---

## 🆕 First-Time Login

If this is your first time logging in.

1. Check the box labeled **“First time login / Need password”**.
2. Click **Send Password**.
3. A password will be emailed to the provided address.

---

## 🔐 Logging In

Once you’ve received your password:

1. Uncheck the **“First time login / Need password”** box.
2. Enter your Vendor Code, Email, and the Password from your email.
3. Click **Login** to access your dashboard.

---

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

- Make sure the **email** matches the one registered by your vendor.
- Passwords are case-sensitive.
- If you don't receive an email, check your **spam folder**.
