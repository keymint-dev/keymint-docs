# Keymint Support

We're here to help you get the most out of Keymint's software licensing platform.

## Support Channels

### 📧 Email Support
- **Primary Contact**: [support@keymint.dev](mailto:support@keymint.dev)
- **Response Time**: 
  - Basic Tier: Up to 48 hours
  - Pro Tier: Within 24 hours
  - Enterprise Tier: Immediate priority support

### 💬 Live Chat
- Available directly in the Keymint Dashboard
- Hours: 9 AM - 6 PM PST, Monday through Friday

### 🌐 Online Resources
- [Documentation](/docs/introduction)
- [FAQs](/help/faqs)
- [API Reference](/api/index)

## Support Tiers

### 🆓 Basic Support
- Email support
- Community forum access
- Standard documentation

### 🚀 Pro Support
- Priority email support
- Live chat access
- Faster response times
- Dedicated troubleshooting

### 🏆 Enterprise Support
- Dedicated support manager
- Immediate priority support
- Custom onboarding
- SLA-backed response times
- Direct phone support

## Troubleshooting Tips

1. **Check Documentation**: Most common issues can be resolved by consulting our comprehensive documentation.
2. **Gather Information**: Before contacting support, prepare:
   - Keymint account details
   - Specific error messages
   - Steps to reproduce the issue
